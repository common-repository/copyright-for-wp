Provides a simple method for webmaster to add copyright in wordpress site, wordpress post, rss feed

== Description ==

Copyright for WP is a simple plugin to help webmaster to add copyright in wordpress site, wordpress post, rss feed

I have a site which created 10 years before, with 1000 original post, in recent days, I found there are some crawel just copy my post to their mobile app and displayed as their original content, removed all my site name, author... and so on.
That's why I develop this plugin to add copyrights in my posts, in my rss feed, and at the bottom of the wordpress site
  
In back end, please check "Copyright" menu, you will find 3 sub-menu iem: 
"Post Copyright",  "Site Copyright", "RSS Copyright" , please just click to open settings panel. 
Then you can opt to display or hide original copyright links in wordpress site, wordpress post, rss feed, and you can custom copyright note copyright editor  

In front end, you will see the copyright under the content of wordpress post, or at the bottom of the wordpress site, or at the bottom of the rss feed 

== Installation ==
1. Download the plugin and click activate.
 

== How To Use ==
1. Login to admin area, and click Copyright menu
2. Click to display or hide original copyright links in wordpress site, wordpress post, rss feed
3. You can custom content of your copyright information via editor in the settings panel
4. In front end, you will see the copyright under the content of wordpress post, or at the bottom of the wordpress site, or at the bottom of the rss feed
